Biography

William Shakespeare (1564-1616) was an English playwright, poet, and actor. He is often regarded as the greatest writer in the English language and the world's greatest dramatist. His extant works, including collaborations, consist of approximately 39 plays, 154 sonnets, and two long narrative poems. His plays have been translated into every major living language and are performed more often than those of any other playwright.

Works

  • Hamlet (1603)
  • Othello (1603)
  • Macbeth (1606)
  • A Midsummer Night's Dream (1595)
